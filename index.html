Introduction

Growing World

Around the world, in order to keep up with global demands, education systems are largely shifting towards modernized styles, and Cambodia is no exception. With new technologies, teaching methods, and standards, classrooms are becoming more interactive, students are taught to think for themselves, think more critically, and are taught to be creative

Preservation

However, as this system grows, there is increasing concern about the loss of traditional values with respect to education. This includes the disappearance of many practices that have molded generations of students and teachers. The transformation brings an important question of whether traditional values still have a meaningful impact on education, and if so, what should be preserved and adapted?
