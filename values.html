Importance of Traditional Values

Before looking into the place of traditional values, it’s important to understand how they have traditionally guided Cambodian education. These aren’t just abstract ideas, they shape how classrooms are run, how students relate to teachers, and even how success is defined.

Importance of Traditional Values

Looking at the benefits and challenges of these values side by side, it is clear that tradition in Cambodian education is not inherently good or bad, rather it depends on context. These values provided stability and moral grounding but they will sometimes limit curiosity or reinforce rigid roles. However, rather than discarding these values, a more valuable action may be to integrate.
